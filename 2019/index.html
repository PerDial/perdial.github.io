The 1st International Workshop on Pervasive Computing and Dialogue Systems (PerDial 2019)

Overview

The 1st International Workshop on Pervasive Computing and Dialogue Systems (PerDial 2018) aims to serve as a platform for cross-disciplinary discussions on ways, problems, challenges and perspectives of mutual integration of Dialogue Systems and Pervasive Computing technologies.
